Lyrics

Verovio supports basic <verse> and <syl> elements. The layout algorithm takes into account the lyrics and spaces the notes and the staves appropriately.

Multiple lines

Verovio also supports multiple lines of lyrics. In that case, each line needs to have a corresponding @n attribute in the <verse> element. The layout algorithm takes into account the multiple lines and spaces the staves appropriately.

Lyric connectors

Verovio supports lyric connectors @con and @wordpos in <syl> elements. The connectors will be appropriately continued in case of system break occurring between the starting and ending node. A full example can be seen in the viewer.

Styling

Verovio offers lyric styling on a per staff basis. @lyric.name, @lyric.style, and @lyric.weight in <staffDef> elements are supported.
