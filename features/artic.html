Articulations

Verovio supports both @artic on notes and <artic> for the encoding articulations. Currently, the articulation values supported are:

  • acc
  • stacc
  • ten
  • stacciss
  • marc
  • marc-stacc
  • spicc
  • ten-stacc
  • dnbow
  • upbow
  • harm
