Score and barlines

Verovio supports the score parameters defined <scoreDef>, <staffGrp> and <staffDef>elements with some of their attributes. Vevorio follows the standard MEI practice for score parameters inheritance: <staffDef> elements inherits parameters defined in the parent <scoreDef> unless if they are overriden. Verovio also supports various types of barline for the <measure> elements.
