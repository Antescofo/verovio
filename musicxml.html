Verovio supports basic conversion from MusicXML to MEI. You can see here the MusicXML example file of a page of a the Beethoven's Lied op. 98 converted on the fly in your browser.

The resulting MEI file can be saved through the MEI button on the top right.

You can also select your own file and try to convert it. You will then be able to save the resulting MEI file.

This feature is highly experimental at this stage. For a more complete conversion from MusicXML to MEI we strongly recommend to use the XSL stylesheet.

Terms of use: the conversion to MEI in performed locally in your browser. This means that the MusicXML data is not uploaded to our server.
